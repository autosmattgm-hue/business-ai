🎯
Structure
The PSMTA Framework

Problem → Solution → Market → Traction → Ask. The proven pitch structure used in the world's top accelerators.

🎬
Opening
Start with a Story

Open with a vivid 30-second story about a real person facing the problem you solve. Stories are 22x more memorable than statistics alone.

📊
Slides
One Idea Per Slide

Each slide should have ONE message. Big visuals, minimal text. If you can't say it in a headline, you haven't thought it through.

👁️
Delivery
The 7-38-55 Rule

7% words, 38% tone of voice, 55% body language. Practise your vocal energy and eye contact as much as your content.

🔢
Numbers
Make Numbers Tangible

Instead of "$1M market" say "1 in every 5 households in our city." Translate abstract figures into human-scale realities.

❓
Q&A
Anticipate Hard Questions

Prepare 10 tough questions investors might ask. Practice saying "That's a great challenge — here's how we think about it..." confidently.
